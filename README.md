## 1. 📋 Headline Type Cheatsheet

| Type                    | Best for                       | Example                                                      |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Professional**        | Corporate, finance, tech       | `Senior Project Manager | PMP Certified | On‑time & under budget` |
| **Creative**            | Marketing, design, startups    | `Turning Coffee into Code | Full‑Stack Developer`            |
| **Direct**              | Sales, operations, engineering | `Sales Manager | $5M+ Revenue | B2B SaaS`                    |
| **Question‑Driven**     | Consulting, coaching, services | `Need a Reliable Recruiter? | 100+ placements/year`          |
| **Achievement‑Focused** | Any (shows results)            | `Increased Sales 40% | Business Development Manager`         |
| **Action‑Oriented**     | Roles that emphasize momentum  | `Building & Scaling 3 Startups | Product & Growth`           |

Pick the type that matches your **industry** and **personality**.

---

## 2. 🔧 How to Turn Any Example into YOUR Headline (3 Steps)

**Step 1 – Swap the role**  
Replace the job title with yours (or your target title).  

**Step 2 – Swap the skills/tools**  
Replace the technical terms with your own top 2–3 hard skills.  

**Step 3 – Swap the result with yours**  
Even a small metric works: “trained 3 people”, “cut costs 5%”, “managed $10k budget”.

**Example transformation:**

| Original (professional)                                      | Your version                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| `Marketing Director | Brand Strategy & Digital Transformation | Driving Revenue Growth` | `Marketing Coordinator | Social Media & Canva | Grew Instagram engagement 30%` |

See? Same structure, personalized.

## 4. 📚 3 Short Case Studies: One Idea, Three Roles

Take a **professional headline formula**: `[Role] | [Key skill 1], [Skill 2] | [Result]`

- **Software Engineer:** `Full‑Stack Engineer | React, Node.js | Shipped 5 features`
- **HR Generalist:** `HR Generalist | Recruitment, Onboarding | Reduced time‑to‑hire by 20%`
- **Teacher:** `High School Math Teacher | Curriculum Design | 95% pass rate on state exam`

Same formula, different execution.

## 5. ⚠️ Avoid These 4 Copy‑Paste Mistakes

| Mistake                                                      | Fix                                         |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| Copying an example exactly (still has “PMP” when you’re not certified) | Replace every credential with your own      |
| Using “I” or “my” in the headline                            | Headlines are not sentences – omit pronouns |
| Forgetting the 220‑character limit                           | Paste into LinkedIn’s edit box to check     |
| Choosing a “creative” headline for a conservative industry   | Match tone to your field (see section 3)    |

## 6. ✍️ Your Headline Idea Worksheet

Fill this in 60 seconds:

1. **My current / target role:** _______________  
2. **Headline type I chose (from section 1):** _______________  
3. **My top 2–3 hard skills:** _______________  
4. **One small win or metric:** _______________  

Now write your headline using the formula from your chosen type.

**Example (creative type):**  
`[Role] + [Fun skill twist] | [Skills]`  
→ `Design Thinker | UX/UI Designer | Crafting digital experiences that delight`
